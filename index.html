Multimedia Portfolio

This is my multimedia portfolio that aims to showcase some of the work I have done over the past 3 years in the Multimedia Technician course.

Within this Portfolio, I will focus on applications from Adobe, specifically Photoshop (image retouching), Illustrator (vectorization), After Effects (motion graphics), and Premiere (video editing). Additionally, I will present a collection of 3D modeling projects with realistic effects created using Blender software. Moreover, I will showcase my photographic endeavors, highlighting various activities undertaken during school projects, meticulously documented through the lens of a camera.
